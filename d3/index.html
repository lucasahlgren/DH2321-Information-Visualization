Project 2 - World values survey

World values survey visualization

This is world values survey visualization including data from gapminder. Here you can learn more about what people think is important in life and average life expectancy data from gapminder. Click on a country to learn more about it. You can also zoom and pan on the map to easily find the country you want.

Besides average life expectancy each category presents the number of percent of people how thinks the category is very important or rather important. The categories are: work, politics, family, religion, happiness, state of health, leisure time and friends. Each category is coded with the following colors:

  • Life expectancy
  • Work
  • Politics
  • Family
  • Religion
  • Happiness
  • State of health
  • Leisure time
  • Friends
